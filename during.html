On That Day

  1. Donor will first need to fill up the blood donation registration form/card.

  2. Have their body weighted.

  3. Have a blood test to identify the blood group and haemoglobin level.

  4. Undergoes pre-donation counselling by Medical Officer / Nurse.

  5. If qualified, have their details registered and blood donation book issued.

  6. This is followed by the blood donating process itself that would normally last between 7-15.

  7. After the donation completed and needle removed, the donor needs to rest for at least 10 minutes before getting off the bed.

  8. Donor will then be served with light refreshment.
