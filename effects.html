Pros & Cons of Blood Donation

Advantages

- Identifying adverse health effects

  • High or low blood pressure
  • Anemia

- Contributing to the community

  • Donating one unit of blood may save the lives of up to three people
  • Reduce iron levels for those with Hemochromatosis to the community

Disadvantages

- Tempory Reactions

Donating blood is safe, as long as the centre follows the standard guidelines

These reactions will disappear within 24 hours:

  • Weakness
  • Diziness
  • Feeling faint/ lightheadedness
  • Nausea
  • Bleeding from the needle prick & under the skin or bruising

- Adverse effects

In rare cases, a person may experience a more severe adverse effect, such as:

  • Low blood pressure
  • Muscle contractions
  • Breathing difficulty
  • Fainting
  • Vomiting
  • Convulsions
